RNA to Protein Translation Tool

Our free RNA to protein translator converts RNA sequences to amino acid sequences using the standard genetic code. The RNA translation tool processes mRNA sequences (containing A, C, G, U nucleotides) and translates them into proteins, simulating the process of protein synthesis that occurs in cells during gene expression.

The RNA translator reads your RNA sequence in the forward reading frame, converting each three-nucleotide codon (UUU, UUC, etc.) into its corresponding amino acid according to the genetic code. This RNA to protein translation is essential for understanding gene expression, analyzing mRNA sequences, and predicting protein products from RNA transcripts. Use our main sequence analyzer for comprehensive analysis, DNA translation tool for DNA sequences, or our protein molecular weight calculator to analyze the translated protein.

All RNA translation is performed instantly in your browser with complete privacy. The RNA to protein translator is essential for molecular biologists, geneticists, and bioinformatics researchers working on gene expression, transcriptomics, and understanding the relationship between RNA sequences and their encoded proteins.
