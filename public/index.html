Sequence Analysis Tools

Our free online sequence analysis tool provides comprehensive multiple sequence analysis capabilities for both DNA and protein sequences. Whether you're performing sequence analysis for research, educational purposes, or bioinformatics projects, this tool offers instant results without requiring any registration or backend processing. Use our GC content calculator for DNA analysis, reverse complement tool for sequence manipulation, or our ORF finder for open reading frame detection.

For DNA sequence analysis, the tool calculates GC content, AT content, provides reverse complement sequences, translates DNA to protein using our DNA translation tool, and detects open reading frames (ORFs). For protein sequence analysis, it computes molecular weight with our protein molecular weight calculator, amino acid composition, hydrophobic percentage, and predicts signal peptides using advanced heuristics. This genome analysis tool is perfect for researchers working on genome assembly projects or studying protein expression systems.

All analysis is performed instantly in your browser using JavaScript - no data is sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for your sequences. Explore our specialized tools for sequence analysis, genome analysis, and protein analysis to enhance your bioinformatics workflow.
